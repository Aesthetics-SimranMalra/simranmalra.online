Meet Simran Malra

Aesthetics By Simran Malra is a specialised skin and aesthetic clinic based in Sirsa, Haryana. Our goal is to help clients achieve healthy, confident skin through personalised treatments and advanced aesthetic procedures.

We believe skin care is not an expense — it is a skin-vestment. Every treatment is designed after proper consultation, ensuring safety, comfort and natural-looking results.
